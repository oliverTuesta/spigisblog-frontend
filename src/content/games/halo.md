> One of the most fun games I’ve ever played.

This game is very linear, but it never feels like it’s holding your hand because you always know what you have to do: eliminate the enemies. It’s simple, and that’s one of the things that makes it so good, the developers turned this straightforward idea into something incredibly enjoyable.

The story and the art are both great. The only downside is that some missions feel repetitive, but everything else is amazing.

I know for a fact that the multiplayer is incredible to play with friends. I used to have LAN matches for hours during my childhood. Until now, though, I had never fully played the campaign. I always saw how obsessed people were with Master Chief and the Halo games, and now I finally understand why.

The final mission with the vehicle was perfect, the music and the whole atmosphere came together to give me goosebumps.

Overall, it’s an excellent game, just slightly longer than it needed to be.
